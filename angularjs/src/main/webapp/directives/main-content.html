Introduction to SMB Nexus

Welcome to SMB Nexus. This is your one stop shop for all your small to medium sized business software needs. We provide a suite of APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) to meet nearly every business need. With new APIs being developed all the time. Chances are if we don't have it, we soon will. Check out our list of current and up coming APIs, here.

We decided to take an API approach to our software development for a number of reasons. Not the least of which is scalability and versatility. APIs allow for nearly any 'front-end' or 'user experience' to be developed, using nearly any technology. Whether you want your application to run on Windows, Macintosh's OS X, Linux, the web, iOS, Android, or even two or more of them. An API can provide the business logic your app needs. Since your user experience is up to you branding is never a problem, nor is mixing and matching APIs to build the perfect application that meets your business needs, without investing in functionality you don't need.

The API approach allows for small businesses to get started with the software they need for a fraction of the cost other business software providers demand. With a little technical know-how, you can build your own application and subscribe to one of our APIs for less than $30.00 dollars a month. Don't have the tech skills or know someone that does? We can provide you with everything from tutorials to a generic low cost front-end, or even a fully custom and branded front-end developed by our expert development team.
